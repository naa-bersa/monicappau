ABOUT MONICA

I have always been driven by curiosity. When something doesn’t make sense, I ask why and what until I understand it fully. That mindset is what led me to UX design.

During a student job, I observed a designer testing a product on users. I became fascinated not just by the interface, but by the thinking behind it, asking questions about her decisions, user testing methods, and how she structured the experience. That conversation introduced me to user experience design and changed the direction of my studies.

I recently graduated from BYU–Idaho, specializing in UX/UI, with experience in user research, interaction design, prototyping, and usability testing, along with a background in graphic design and web development.

I focus on uncovering real user needs and creating solutions that are intuitive and purposeful. I am now looking for opportunities to apply my skills and passion to craft meaningful, human-centered experiences.
